Ford Credit

Credit Application

Applying for credit online is convenient and secure. The process usually takes around 10-15 minutes and you can expect to receive a response right away.
You’ll be asked to provide the following basic personal information:
  • Social Security Number
  • Date of Birth
  • Employment status and/or annual income
  • Address and whether you own or rent
  • Monthly mortgage or rent
  • Email address
You'll also be asked to answer a few questions about your preferred vehicle and financing options (i.e. financing type: purchase or lease, and application type: individual or joint). Your final vehicle and financing options will be selected at your local dealer.
